download audio, video, and other types of files from various websites and social networks: youtube.

1st way: Enter the URL-address in the input field at the top of the main page and press Enter or
click on the button to the right of the input field.

2nd way: Add "savefrom.net/" or "sfrom.net/" before the URL and press Enter. example:
sfrom.net/http://youtube.com/watch?v=u7deClndzQw

3rd way: Use short domain names: ssyoutube.com.
